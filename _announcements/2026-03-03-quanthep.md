Announcement: QuantHEP 2026
Event page: <https://indico.global/event/16939/>

We are pleased to announce the 2026 edition of QuantHEP.

QuantHEP 2026, to be held at Queen Mary University of London, Mile End campus, London, marks the fourth edition of the QuantHEP conference series (quanthep.org). The meeting brings together researchers and students with a shared interest in applying quantum technologies to high‑energy physics, including quantum simulation, quantum computation, and advanced numerical methods for probing high‑energy phenomena. 

The Registration deadline is 20th June, submission deadlines for talk and poster abstracts are 15th April and 13th June, respectively. 

Full information, including the preliminary programme and registration instructions, is available on the Indico page. We would be grateful if you could share this announcement within your department or research network.
